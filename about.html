Who We Are

Empowering South Texas residents to build communities that represent them through fact-based policies. We can shape our future together.

Changing the world is possible.

Our party is growing and unifying! During these unprecedented times affecting the world, our nation, and community, it’s more important than ever to stand up for our American values of freedom, family, and community.

We work with all members of the South Texas community to ensure that Democrats are elected to all levels of government while building unity and strength within the party and our local community.

County Chairman

Our Hidalgo County Democratic Party Chairman is dedicated to expanding our grassroots network, overseeing daily operations, and leading the strategy to elect Democrats up and down the ballot throughout the region.

Precinct Chairs

Precinct Chairs are the backbone of the party. They serve as the face of the Democratic Party in their immediate neighborhood. We have opportunities available for you to step up and lead your precinct!
