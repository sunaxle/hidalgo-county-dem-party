This is our community accountability board. When extreme politicians or bad actors push harmful policies, spread misinformation, or ignore the needs of South Texas, we call it out.

Education March 12, 2026

The Private School Voucher Scam

State leadership continues to push taxpayer-funded vouchers that would defund our local Hidalgo County public schools. South Texas rural and urban districts alike stand to lose millions in funding, forcing teacher layoffs and larger class sizes, all to subsidize private academies mostly located in wealthy suburbs.

Healthcare March 08, 2026

Refusal to Expand Medicaid

Texas has the highest number of uninsured residents in the nation, and Hidalgo County specifically suffers from a severe lack of healthcare access. Despite the federal government offering to cover 90% of the cost, our state leaders refuse to expand Medicaid, leaving hard-working South Texans without access to life-saving doctors and preventative care.

Infrastructure March 01, 2026

Grid Instability & Utility Rate Hikes

Corporate utility monopolies continue to hike rates on everyday families while failing to winterize the Texas electrical grid. Record profits are being funneled to executives while working-class neighborhoods experience rolling blackouts during extreme weather. We demand a reliable grid and an end to price gouging.
