The Issues Data Map

Hover over the nodes to explore the interconnected policy priorities facing South Texas Democrats from Capitol Hill down to your local precinct.

Federal / National
Voting Rights
John Lewis VRA
Federal protection is required to counter state-level suppression efforts targeting minority and young voters in South Texas.
Border Commerce
$300B+
Annual trade passes through Texas ports of entry. We advocate for smart border security that doesn't disrupt vital economic lifelines.
Medicare Expansion
1 in 5
Texans are uninsured. Federal incentives must be leveraged fully to force state leadership to accept healthcare expansion for working families.
The Wealth Gap
Monopolies
Billionaires and unchecked corporate greed hold too much power. We must empower the working class over those who monopolize and shut out democracy.
Veterans Services
D.O.G.E. Cuts
Working-class families and veterans who rely on government employment and specific services are being harmed by federal budget slashes.
SAVE Act
Opposition
We strongly oppose the so-called SAVE Act, which operates as a transparent trap designed to place unnecessary burdens on our communities.
State (Texas)
Public Ed Funding
Interference
We must defeat voucher schemes and fight back against state mandates dictating curriculum to conform with a white nationalist agenda.
Housing Crisis
Renters
Renters and homeowners face extraordinary challenges. We need local representation that fights for affordable housing over corporate land grabs.
Reproductive Freedom
Access
Fighting draconian state laws to restore fundamental healthcare access and bodily autonomy for millions of Texas women.
Grid Reliability
ERCOT
Holding state leadership accountable for the failing power grid and ensuring working families aren't burdened with corporate failures.
Hidalgo County
Property Taxes
Relief
Working with the County Appraisal District to ensure fair valuations and promoting homestead exemptions to keep families in their homes.
Regional Drainage
Bond Props
Prioritizing county-wide infrastructure bonds to fix the aging drainage systems that cause severe flooding during hurricane season.
