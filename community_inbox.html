Community Inbox & Pain Points

We believe in transparency and listening to our neighbors. Below is a log of anonymous feedback, inquiries, and pain points submitted directly by Hidalgo County residents.

Submit Anonymous Feedback
Infrastructure
The constant flooding in east McAllen during heavy rains is becoming unbearable. It feels like every time we get a storm, the drainage systems completely fail. What are local officials doing to actually fix the underlying pipes rather than just patching the roads?
Anonymous Resident March 2026

Admin response:

Thank you for bringing this up. The party is actively supporting local bond measures aimed at overhauling the Precinct 2 drainage systems. We are hosting a town hall on this exact topic next month.

Voting Access
I noticed that the polling location near the university was closed during the primary. A lot of students don't have cars and rely on that spot. We need more early voting locations on campus!
UTRGV Student February 2026
Healthcare
With the cost of living going up, the lack of affordable healthcare options in rural parts of the county is a crisis. People are driving an hour just to afford basic pediatric care. Please make healthcare expansion a priority in Austin.
Anonymous Parent January 2026

Admin response:

We hear you. Expanding Medicaid in Texas remains a top priority for our state delegations. Please consider joining our upcoming letter-writing campaign to the Governor's office.

General Inquiry
I want to get involved with blockwalking but I work late shifts and weekends are usually my only free time. Do you have training sessions or groups that go out on Sunday afternoons?
Anonymous Resident December 2025

Admin response:

Yes! We have a dedicated Sunday "Weekend Warriors" crew. Check our Events calendar to RSVP for the next Sunday afternoon session.
