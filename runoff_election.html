Tuesday, May 26th

Primary Runoff Election

No candidate secured 50% of the vote during the March Primary in the following races. It is critical that Hidalgo County Democrats return to the polls on May 26th to definitively select our nominees for the General Election and for party leadership.

HD 41 Texas State Representative District 41

The seat for State Representative District 41 is open following Rep. Bobby Guerra's tenure. This runoff will determine the Democratic nominee for the Rio Grande Valley.

Seby Haddad

McAllen Mayor Pro Tem & City Commissioner

Victor "Seby" Haddad currently serves as a McAllen City Commissioner (District 5). A community banker, his campaign focuses on driving local economic development, improving infrastructure, and advocating for robust public education funding across the Rio Grande Valley. Endorsed by outgoing Rep. Bobby Guerra.

Campaign Website
VS

Julio Salinas

Co-Chair, TDP Hispanic Caucus

Julio Salinas brings extensive Texas Legislature experience as a dedicated staffer and leader within the Texas Democratic Party Hispanic Caucus. His grassroots platform emphasizes making the Rio Grande Valley affordable for working families, defending public schools from voucher schemes, and restoring community trust.
