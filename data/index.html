1. Bayesian Shrinkage & The "Turnout Premium"

2. Confounding Variables: Regional Coattails vs Local Organizers

If a highly-contested Commissioner Race is entirely responsible for the turnout wave, then Contested and Vacant precincts inside that same District will perform identically to each other. By applying the master geographic mapping array across all precincts, we calculated the true gap. Commissioner Pct 3 is the perfect microcosm. While the highly competitive race lifted the general Vacant baseline to 19.3%, the raw infrastructure of active, door-knocking precinct chairs dragged their own sub-neighborhoods up to an explosive 26.08% turnout—yielding a massive +6.7% localized swing.
